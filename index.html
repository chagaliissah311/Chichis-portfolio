About Immaculate

More than a runway presence, she is a voice of elegance, grace, and service.

Immaculate Chichi is a modern model whose work effortlessly blends fashion intelligence with emotional depth. From high-fashion editorials to stage-ready runway moments, her presence is rooted in versatility, poise, and a captivating sense of storytelling.

She was crowned Miss Curvy UoE 2026/2027, featured on the cover of Pulse Magazine, and continues to shape cultural conversations through both glamour and purpose. Her work extends beyond the lens into community outreach, where she champions confidence and giving back through meaningful initiatives.

"More than a model — a voice for the community."
